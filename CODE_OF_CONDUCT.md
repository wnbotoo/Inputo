# Code of Conduct

Inputo should be a welcoming project for contributors with different backgrounds, experience levels, and communication styles. We expect everyone participating in issues, pull requests, discussions, reviews, and community spaces to help keep the project respectful and productive.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, patient, and direct.
- Assume good intent while still naming risks clearly.
- Give actionable feedback focused on the work.
- Respect privacy, security, and confidentiality boundaries.
- Make room for newer contributors to ask questions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, threats, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Demeaning language related to identity, background, or experience level.
- Sexualized language or unwanted attention.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Sharing secrets, private prompts, sensitive screenshots, or exploit details in public project spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close conversations, reject contributions, temporarily limit participation, or ban participants when needed to protect the community and project.

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, contact a maintainer privately through the repository owner profile. Include relevant links, screenshots, and context when safe to do so. Maintainers will handle reports with care and limit disclosure to people who need the information to respond.
